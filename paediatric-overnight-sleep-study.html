Payment of your overnight sleep study and cancelling or changing your appointment

  • Payment: There is a facility fee of $410.00 to be paid for the sleep study.  This fee is due in full upon arrival for your sleep study. Please note that this is an out-of-pocket expense and cannot be claimed from Medicare or a private health fund. If you are a Centrelink Health Care card holder, there is a facility fee of $160.00 to be paid for the sleep study.
  • Cancellation or Change: Please provide at least 2 working days’ notice if you need to cancel or change the sleep study booking. This will enable a person on our waiting list to be contacted for testing. If you do not provide this notice, you will be charged an out-of-pocket cancellation fee of $100.00.
