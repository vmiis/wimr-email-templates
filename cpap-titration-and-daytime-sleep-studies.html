Payment of your sleep studies and cancelling or changing your appointment

  • Payment: There is a facility fee of $630.00 to be paid for your CPAP Titration and Daytime sleep studies. If you have been advised that a CPAP education appointment is required, then an additional fee of $75.00 is payable, for a total of $685.00. If you do not provide this notice, you will be charged an out-of-pocket cancellation fee of $200.00.
